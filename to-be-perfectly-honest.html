To Be Perfectly Honest

October 2025

To Be Perfectly Honest marks a welcome restart after a long hiatus from writing and recording music. Songs were written and recorded from Jan through Sep 2025. Special nods to School of Song sessions with Brian Eno and A.G. Cook for getting the still boulder rolling downhill. All performances, tracking, mixing, and mastering were done by Walt from his home studio in Cleveland, OH.
